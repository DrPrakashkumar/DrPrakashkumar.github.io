Assistant Professor · Production Engineering · NIT Tiruchirappalli

I work at the intersection of vibration & nonlinear dynamics, human–structure interaction, and advanced welding & additive manufacturing. My recent work spans modelling of pedestrian-induced forces on structures and microstructural–mechanical integrity of dissimilar welds (Inconel 718 · ASS 304L), including laser/arc welding and WAAM.
