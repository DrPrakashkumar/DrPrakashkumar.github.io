About

Profile

Dr. Prakash Kumar is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Production Engineering at the National Institute of Technology, Tiruchirappalli. His research focuses on vibration and control, nonlinear dynamics, modelling of human-induced forces on structures, and welding and additive manufacturing.
